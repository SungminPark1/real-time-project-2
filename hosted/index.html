Controls

Movement: WASD

Skills: Spacebar

Alive Players: Push bombs away from you (strength depends on distance)

Dead Players: Place a bomb where you are

Goal/Tips

Score only increases when your alive.

The growing transparent grey circles indicates the time left to get out of the explosion.

Only explosions (red circles) can kill you.

Players can only collide with other living players.

Dead players have shorter cooldown on their skill.

You can not see other player's cooldown. (blue ring around your character)
